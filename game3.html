How to Play

  1. Click the "Start" button
  2. Wait for the signal to change from "Wait..." to "CLICK!"
  3. Click as fast as possible when you see "CLICK!"
  4. Your reaction time will be displayed in milliseconds
  5. Try multiple times to beat your best score!
  6. Be careful: Clicking too early counts as a failed attempt

💡 Tips for Better Scores

  • Keep your hand relaxed and ready on the mouse/touchpad
  • Focus on the display area and stay alert
  • Avoid clicking too early - patience is key!
  • Average human reaction time is 200-300ms
  • Professional gamers average 150-200ms
