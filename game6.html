How to Play

  • Click Start Game to begin.
  • Watch the colors light up and listen to the sounds.
  • When the sequence is finished, repeat it back by clicking the colors in the exact same order.
  • Each round, a new color is added to the end of the sequence.
  • One mistake and it's game over!
