⭕ Tic Tac Toe

Get three in a row to win!

How to Play

  • Player 1: Mark your moves with X
  • Player 2: Mark your moves with O
  • Take turns clicking on empty squares
  • First player to get 3 marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins!
  • If all 9 squares are filled with no winner, it's a draw
  • Results are saved automatically to the dashboard

💡 Strategy Tips

The center square (position 5) is the most strategic. Control the center and corners to increase your chances of winning!
